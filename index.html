About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. I earned my PhD degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2024.


My current research interests focus on exploring reinforcement learning techniques for training LLM-based agents deployed in real-world long-horizon agentic applications. Please refer to [verl-agent] for more details.
